Frequently Asked Questions

Are these CUET notes really free? ▾
Yes, all Thunderstudy notes are completely free. There is no subscription, no login wall, and no paid tier. Every subject's notes can be accessed directly via the links above.
Which syllabus do these notes follow? ▾
All notes are based strictly on the NCERT Class 12 syllabus as prescribed by NTA for CUET UG. They cover only the chapters and units that appear in the official CUET syllabus for each subject.
What is the difference between Detail Notes and Short Notes? ▾
Detail Notes are full chapter explanations with definitions, examples, diagrams, and in-depth coverage — suitable for first-time reading. Short Notes are condensed quick-revision summaries covering only the key points, formulas, and must-remember facts — ideal for last-minute revision before the CUET exam.
How do I practice after reading notes? ▾
Open the Thunderstudy Dashboard at commercesehoga.github.io/cuet and take chapter-wise CBT mock tests for each chapter you studied. The platform uses the official CUET scoring pattern (+5 for correct, −1 for wrong) and saves your scores automatically.
Who made these notes? ▾
Thunderstudy was created by Wondermayank (Mayank), a developer and content creator dedicated to helping Indian Class 12 students crack CUET across all streams. The platform is maintained actively and updated for every CUET cycle.
Are notes available for CUET 2025 and 2026? ▾
Yes. The notes are aligned with the current NTA CUET syllabus and are updated regularly. The platform also includes PYQs (Previous Year Questions) from CUET 2023, 2024, and 2025 in the mock test section.

About Thunderstudy

What is Thunderstudy?

Thunderstudy is a completely free CUET preparation platform for Class 12 students in India. It covers all streams — Commerce, Science, Humanities, English, GAT, Computer Science, Informatics Practices, and Physical Education — through chapter-wise CBT mock tests, score analytics, a live class rank leaderboard, and free study notes based on the NCERT Class 12 syllabus.

The platform follows the official NTA CUET scoring pattern (+5 for correct answers, −1 for wrong answers). Students can track their chapter-wise performance, compare class ranks, and earn XP and achievement badges through a gamified learning system — all at zero cost.
