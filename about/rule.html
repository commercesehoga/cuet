Official Exam Day Carry Guide

What to Carry &
What Not to Carry

Know exactly what to bring to your CUET 2026 exam centre — and what is strictly banned. Stay prepared, avoid disqualification.

📅 11–31 May 2026 🖥️ CBT Mode 🏛️ All Centres ⚠️ Strict Frisking
🚨
Highly Sensitive Metal Detectors at Entry

All candidates will be subjected to compulsory frisking with metal detectors before entering the exam centre. Any banned item found = you may be denied entry or booked under Unfair Means (UFM).

✅ Must Carry

Items You Must Bring

Without these, you will NOT be allowed to sit in the exam

🖨️
Admit Card Printout

A4 size clear printout with Self-Declaration (Undertaking) duly filled in. Downloaded from the NTA website.

✅ Mandatory
🪪
Original Photo ID

Must be original, valid, and non-expired — Aadhaar / E-Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / School ID / Class 12 Admit Card / Bank Passbook with photo.

✅ Any One
📸
Passport-Size Photograph

Same photo as uploaded in the online application form. To be pasted on the attendance sheet in the exam room.

✅ 1 Photo
🖊️
Simple Transparent Ball Point Pen

Only a simple, transparent ball-point pen is allowed. No gel pens, pencils, or multi-color pens.

✅ Transparent only
♿
PwD / PwBD Certificate

If claiming relaxation under PwD/PwBD category — carry the certificate issued by the authorized medical officer.

✅ If Applicable
🍌
Sugar / Fruits (Diabetic Only)

Diabetic candidates may carry sugar tablets or fruits like banana, apple, or orange inside the centre.

✅ Diabetic Only
⏰
Report 2 Hours Before the Exam!

Candidates wearing religious / customary / cultural dress should report at least 2 hours before the last reporting time so enough time is available for proper frisking.

📋
Admit Cards are NOT sent by post

Download and print your admit card from the NTA website: https://cuet.nta.nic.in — no physical card will be dispatched.

🚫 Strictly Banned

Items NOT Allowed Inside

These are completely prohibited — no exceptions

📱
Mobile Phone

Any mobile, pager, or communication device

🎧
Earphones / Bluetooth

All wireless & audio communication devices

⌚
Watch / Smartwatch

Wristwatch, bracelet, or any timing device

🧮
Calculator

Any calculator or slide rules / log tables

💼
Handbag / Purse

No bags, pouches, or wallets of any kind

🕶️
Goggles / Sunglasses

No eyewear other than prescription glasses

📏
Scale / Geometry Box

Pen, pencil, eraser, scale — not allowed

💾
Pen Drive / DocuPen

No USB drives, electronic pens or scanners

📷
Camera / Tape Recorder

No cameras or recording devices of any kind

💍
Ornaments / Metal Items

Rings, bangles, chains, pins — all banned

🥤
Food / Water Bottles

No packed or open food items or water

🎽
Belt / Cap / Hat

No belts, caps, hats, or metallic headwear

📄
Written / Printed Paper

No notes, textual material, or chit papers

📡
Health Band / Microchip

Any device capable of wireless communication

🎯 Quick Rules

Inside the Exam Hall

Key rules to follow once you enter the exam room

Do This
  • ✓Sit only on the seat allotted to you (Roll Number indicated)
  • ✓Show admit card + ID on demand to invigilator
  • ✓Paste your photo on the attendance sheet
  • ✓Click Save & Next after every question
  • ✓Write your name & application number on rough sheet
  • ✓Submit rough sheet to invigilator before leaving
  • ✓Report any technical issue to invigilator immediately
Never Do This
  • ✗Do NOT change your seat on your own
  • ✗Do NOT talk or communicate with other candidates
  • ✗Do NOT use any electronic device inside the hall
  • ✗Do NOT take rough sheet outside the exam room
  • ✗Do NOT look at another candidate's screen
  • ✗Do NOT submit multiple application forms
  • ✗Do NOT arrive late — late entry is not permitted
