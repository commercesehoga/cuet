Exam Day – Step by Step What to do from morning to finish
1
Night Before
Prepare everything in advance

Keep your admit card, photo ID, passport photo, and ball-point pen ready. Check your exam center location and plan your route.

Admit CardPhoto IDPassport Photo
2
2 Hours Before Exam
Reach the exam center early

Report at least 2 hours before the exam. Candidates wearing religious/customary dress must report 2 hours before last reporting time for proper frisking.

Frisking with metal detectorDocument check
3
At Entry
Show your documents & get seated

Show admit card + photo ID at entry. Find your allotted seat using your Roll Number. Do NOT sit in any other seat — this may cancel your candidature.

Sit in assigned seat onlyPaste photo on attendance sheet
4
Before Exam Starts
Login & verify your question paper

Login with your credentials. Verify the subject on screen matches your admit card. Read all instructions carefully on screen. If subject is wrong, immediately tell the invigilator.

Check subject on screenRead CBT instructions
5
During Exam (60 Minutes)
Answer wisely — use "Mark for Review"

Use Save & Next to save answers. Use Mark for Review if you want to revisit a question later. You can switch between questions anytime. The timer is on the top-right corner of your screen. Exam ends automatically when timer hits zero.

Save & Next for each answerCan change answers anytimeRough sheet provided
6
After Exam Ends
Submit rough sheet to invigilator

Hand over your rough sheet to the invigilator before leaving. Write your name and application number on top of the rough sheet. Do NOT take it outside the hall.

Return rough sheetDo not swap rough sheets

Do's & Don'ts Key rules for the exam
✅ DO These
  • ✓
    Carry a printed admit card (A4 size) with self-declaration filled
  • ✓
    Carry original valid Photo ID (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport etc.)
  • ✓
    Carry 1 passport-size photo (same as uploaded) to paste on attendance sheet
  • ✓
    Use only a simple transparent ball-point pen
  • ✓
    Sit in your allotted seat with your Roll Number
  • ✓
    Save your answer using "Save & Next" after each question
  • ✓
    Report technical issues to the invigilator immediately
  • ✓
    Diabetic candidates can carry sugar tablets / banana / apple / orange
❌ DON'T Do These
  • ✗
    Do NOT carry mobile phone, earphones, Bluetooth, smartwatch
  • ✗
    Do NOT carry calculator, pen drive, or any electronic device
  • ✗
    Do NOT carry wallet, handbag, belt, cap, or goggles
  • ✗
    Do NOT wear full-sleeve heavy clothes or shoes
  • ✗
    Do NOT wear any watch, bracelet, or metallic jewellery
  • ✗
    Do NOT change your seat on your own
  • ✗
    Do NOT communicate with other candidates during exam
  • ✗
    Do NOT take rough sheet outside the exam hall

Dress Code Official CUET dress rules
✅ Wear This
  • ✓
    Light-colored / pastel clothes (peach, baby blue, light pink)
  • ✓
    Half-sleeve tops, kurtas, or plain shirts
  • ✓
    Loose-fitting trousers or regular-fit jeans
  • ✓
    Slippers or sandals with low heels
  • ✓
    Simple hair (tied or open) — no accessories
  • ✓
    Minimal light makeup (girls only)
❌ Avoid This
  • ✗
    Heavy clothes or full sleeves with big buttons/zippers
  • ✗
    Shoes (all candidates)
  • ✗
    High heels (girls)
  • ✗
    Hair accessories — bands, clips, pins, metallic items
  • ✗
    All jewelry — earrings, bangles, rings, chains, anklets
  • ✗
    Cap, hat, or any headgear (boys)
ℹ️
Customary / Religious Dress?

If you come in cultural or religious attire, report at least 2 hours before the last reporting time for proper frisking by the exam staff.

How to Use the CBT Interface Computer Based Test tips
Question Palette (Right Side)

Shows status of all questions — Visited, Not Answered, Answered, Marked for Review

Save & Next

Always click "Save & Next" after answering a question to save your response

Mark for Review

Mark a question to revisit later. Answered + Marked questions ARE counted for evaluation

Clear Response

To change an answer, click "Clear Response" and then select a new option

Auto Submit

Exam ends automatically when the timer reaches zero. No need to submit manually

Computer Malfunction?

Inform invigilator immediately. A new system will be provided and lost time adjusted

Important Reminders Don't miss these
🚨
Late Entry = No Entry

Any candidate reporting after the gate closing time will NOT be allowed inside. Do not be late!

🚨
Multiple Application Forms = Cancellation

Submitting more than one application form will cancel your result. Apply only once.

⚠️
Appear Only at Your Allotted Center

Appearing from any other center — or allowing someone else to write for you — will lead to result cancellation and possible debarment up to 3 years.

💡
CUET Score is valid only for 2026–27

Your NTA Score is valid for admission to Central Universities and other participating universities for academic year 2026-27 only.
