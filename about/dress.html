Dress Code for Girls & Boys

Know exactly what to wear and what to avoid on exam day. Stay prepared, stay confident.

Allowed — Wear These
  • Light-colored / pastel clothes (peach, baby blue, light pink)
  • Half-sleeve tops, kurtas or plain shirts
  • Simple trousers or jeans (not tight-fitting)
  • Flat sandals or jutis (no heels)
  • Minimal, light makeup only (girls)
  • Simple hair — tied or open, no accessories
Not Allowed — Avoid These
  • Tight or body-hugging clothes
  • Skirts / dresses above the knee
  • Full-sleeve or designer outfits with large buttons/zippers
  • All hair accessories (bands, clips, pins, metallic items)
  • All jewelry — earrings, bangles, rings, chains, anklets
  • Mehandi, heavy makeup, watches, metal belts, heels, caps
